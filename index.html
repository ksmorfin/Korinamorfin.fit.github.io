Personal Trainer

I am Korina Morfin, an internationally certified NASM Personal Trainer dedicated to helping clients build strength, confidence, knowledge, and -most importantly- real results at any fitness level. My foundation is smart training: workouts that not only make you sweat, but also respect your process, your technique, and your lifestyle.

I take a science-based, biomechanics-driven approach, designing personalized programs that teach you how to train intelligently, progress safely, and understand how your body moves so you can achieve your goals effectively and sustainably. I do not create generic routines-every plan I design has a purpose and a strategy made specifically for you.

My mission is to guide you step by step so you can discover how strong you can truly become, both physically and mentally. I want you to progress, surprise yourself, maintain your results through healthy habits, and most of all, learn to enjoy the process

Training with me means having a coach who pushes you, corrects you, and supports you through every stage-so you can become a more disciplined, confident, and stronger version of yourself.

Why should you sleep 7 to 8 hours a day?

How important is to eat healthy?

Eating healthy is crucial for health, as it helps prevent chronic diseases such as diabetes, heart disease and cancer, in addition to protecting against malnutrition. Adequate nutrition provides the necessary energy for day to day, improves physical and mental well-being, strengthens teeth and bones, and is one of the main pillars of a long and healthy life...

Does the technique matter?

The technique is important in the gym because it prevents injuries, optimizes the results of the training and allows greater control and efficiency in each exercise. A good technique ensures that the desired muscles are worked, avoiding excessive tension in joints and bones, and maximizing muscle growth and performance...
